F.A.Q

Is Rise Open-Source

Yes, Rise is open source. It is linked in the "More Info" section above.

What is Rise's Prefix

Rise's prefix is "+". To get a list of his commands use "+help".

Does Rise have Slash Commands

Currently no, but in the near future we will need to add them due to discord moving to slash commands for bots.
